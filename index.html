Quick questions

Is the free plan actually enough?

Yes — if you’re the only caretaker, it’s the complete product: unlimited pets, all reminders, logging, offline sync, and full history. You only need Penny Household to share with others.

Do the people I invite have to pay?

No. Only the household owner subscribes, and that covers every member they invite.

What if we lose signal?

Penny works offline. Logs are saved on your device and sync automatically when you’re back online — nothing gets lost.
