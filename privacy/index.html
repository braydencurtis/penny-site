Privacy Policy

Last updated July 14, 2026

Penny is a shared pet-care tracker made for households. To do its job it needs some information about you, your household, and your pets — and nothing more. Three things up front:

We do not sell your data.
We do not use it for advertising.
We do not track you across other apps.

Data we collect

CategoryWhat it includes
AccountYour email address, display name, and Apple/Google sign-in identifiers. Passwords are handled by our authentication provider — we never see them.
HouseholdHousehold name, its members, their roles, and invite codes.
Pets & carePet names, species, birthdates, notes, and photos; care routines (meals, meds, litter); medication name, dosage, and form; and a log of completed tasks including who completed them and when.
PhotosPet and member photos you choose to upload. Entirely optional.
NotificationsA device push token, your notification preferences, and your household’s time zone.
SubscriptionYour subscription status and entitlement, from Apple and our subscription manager. We never receive or store card or payment details — Apple handles payment.
DiagnosticsCrash reports and technical data (device model, OS version, app state at the time of a crash), used only to fix bugs.

What we do not collect

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Precise location — we only store a timezone name (like “America/Chicago”)
  • Your contacts
  • Browsing history
  • Payment card details

Sharing within your household

Penny is built for sharing — within your household only. Your pets, care routines, and the care log (including your name on tasks you complete) are visible to the other members of your household. New members are added only with an admin’s approval.

Retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can delete your account in the app via Me → profile → Delete account, or by emailing us. Care-log history is preserved for the household, so the shared record stays intact for its remaining members.

Security

All data travels over HTTPS/TLS. Database-level access control ensures only members of your household can read your household’s data.

Children

Penny is not directed to children under 13, and we don’t knowingly collect their data.

Your rights

You can access, correct, export, or delete your data. Most of this is doable directly in the app; for anything else, email us and we’ll help.

Changes to this policy

When we update this policy, we’ll update the date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Write to us at support@pennytracker.pet.
